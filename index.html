About

Dr Nick Cartwright is a Senior Lecturer in the School of Engineering and Built Environment, Griffith University, Gold Coast, Australia. As a member of the Civil Engineering discipline, he develops and teaches courses in coastal engineering and catchment hydrology.

This site links to some interactive, web-based resources developed by Nick to support teaching and research activities in the fields of hydrology and coastal science.
