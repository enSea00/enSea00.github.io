Nick's Web Tools

Dr Nick Cartwright is a Senior Lecturer in the School of Engineering and Built Environment at Griffith University, Gold Coast Campus, Australia.
His research expertise falls under the umbrella of coastal processes and encompasses research fields such as: coastal groundwater dynamics, ocean wave dynamics and climatology, climate change influences on coastal dynamics.

This page links to some of the web-based resources that Nick has developed to support teaching and research activities.

Teaching Calculation Applets

An (Interactive) Introduction to Hydrology

A series of interactive calculation applets to explore the processes associated with the Hydrological Cycle and other applied hydrology methods such as flood routing and flood frequency analysis.

An (Interactive) Introduction to Linear Wave Theory

An interactive calculation applet to explore the linear wave theory framework to estimate water wave properties including celerity, kinematics, pressure and eneregy.
