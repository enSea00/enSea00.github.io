About

Dr Nick Cartwright is a coastal scientist with over 20 years experience conducting research and teaching in the fields of coastal processes and catchment hydrology. In particular, Nick has published numerous peer reviewed papers on coastal groundwater dynamics, ocean waves, coastal morphodynamics, regional hydrogeology and hydrology (see Publications page).

This site links to some interactive, web-based resources developed by Nick to support teaching and research activities in the fields of hydrology and coastal science.
